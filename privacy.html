Privacy & Data Handling Policy

Last updated: September 25, 2025

Apex Data ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with the utmost care. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and deletion of your information when you use our service.

1. Data We Collect

To provide our service, we collect the following information:

  • Client Email Address: To deliver the final report and communicate with you regarding your order.
  • Uploaded Data File: The raw data you provide for analysis. This may include sales data, financial figures, user metrics, or any other information you choose to upload.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To perform the data analysis you requested as per the selected service package.
  • To generate and deliver your insight report to the provided email address.
  • We will NEVER use your data for model training, advertising, third-party sales, or any other purpose.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transfers between your browser and our servers are encrypted using industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS).
  • Encryption at Rest: Your uploaded data files are stored in an encrypted state on our secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud Platform).
  • Access Control: Access to your data is strictly limited to the automated systems required to generate your report. Human access is prohibited unless required for explicit, user-requested support.

4. Data Deletion and Retention Policy

We believe in data minimization and do not store your sensitive information longer than necessary.

  • Your uploaded raw data file is permanently and securely deleted from our systems 24 hours after your final insight report has been successfully delivered to your email.
  • Your email address and the fact that a transaction occurred may be retained for longer for our business records, but the sensitive raw data is expunged.

5. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

All data you submit is treated as strictly confidential. By using our service, you agree to our terms, and we, in turn, are bound by a commitment of non-disclosure regarding your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data handling practices, please contact us at contact.apexdata@gmail.com.
