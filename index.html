Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you support?

We currently support .csv, .xlsx (Excel), and .json files. If you have another format, please contact us first.

How do you handle my data's privacy?

Your data is encrypted at all times and permanently deleted from our systems 24 hours after your report is delivered. You can read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I request specific metrics or KPIs?

Yes! Our Pro plans and above include the option for custom metric tracking. You can specify your needs in the upload form.

What is your refund policy?

Due to the digital nature of our service, we do not offer refunds once work has begun. However, if you are unsatisfied with your report, please contact us and we will work with you to make it right.
